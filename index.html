The Library of Closed Art Libraries

About

The Library of Closed Art Libraries is a companion website to the research project Final Chapters: How Academic Art Librarians Navigate Institutional Closure. Situated within the broader crisis in higher education, the project explores the experiences of academic art librarians who have gone through the closure of their institutions, and the practical, ethical, and emotional dimensions of those experiences. It collects lessons learned with the hope that they might be useful to others going through future library closures.

---

Becky Alexander is an archivist at the San Francisco Art Institute Legacy Foundation + Archive, and a librarian at City College of San Francisco. This project grew out of her experience of the 2022 closure of the San Francisco Art Institute, where she worked for 17 years, and her desire to talk to other librarians who had gone through similar experiences.
